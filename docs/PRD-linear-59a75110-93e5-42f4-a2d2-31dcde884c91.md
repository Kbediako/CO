## Summary
- Problem Statement: under concurrent provider-worker pressure, local Node processes can hit OOM or resource exhaustion while same-issue child-lane retries continue to produce provenance-invalid failure output and progress churn. Without explicit host-safety backpressure and provenance-safe progress truth, operators can see noisy retry/progress signals that imply work moved forward even though child-lane provenance failed closed.
- Desired Outcome: make CO-299 a bounded parent implementation lane that detects local Node OOM/resource pressure as host-safety pressure, applies observable backpressure before launching more local provider work, keeps same-issue child-lane and child-stream provenance strict and fail-closed, and prevents retry/progress surfaces from overstating progress after provenance-invalid failures.
