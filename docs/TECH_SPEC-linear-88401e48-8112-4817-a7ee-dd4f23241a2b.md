## Fallback Expiry Contract
- Temporary fallbacks on named high-churn control surfaces get a 14-day review cap and a 30-day maximum lifetime.
- General temporary repo fallbacks get a 30-day review cap and a 60-day maximum lifetime.
- Security/auth/PII/customer/financial/production-impact fallbacks get a 7-day review cap and a 14-day maximum lifetime.
- External migration bridges may last up to 90 days only with reviewer approval, owner issue, deprecation plan, and validation evidence.
- Expired fallbacks block review handoff unless removed, refreshed through a new issue-quality review, or moved to a real blocked state with dependency evidence.
