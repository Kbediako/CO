- Problem Statement: provider-worker validation follow-up `CO-54` calls out two test hermeticity regressions that should stay local-fixture-scoped: `orchestrator/tests/UserConfigStageSets.test.ts` must not be able to drift back to host/provider snapshot state, and `tests/cli-frontend-test.spec.ts` must not rely on repo-root build/guardrail assets that are absent inside its temp workspace fixture.
