## Summary
- Problem Statement: control-host-launched provider-worker manifests can keep `provider_launch_source=null`, `provider_control_host_task_id=null`, and `provider_control_host_run_id=null`, even when the live environment matches the active control-host owner. That leaves `linear child-lane` and `linear child-stream` failing closed on `provider_worker_child_lane_provenance_invalid` / child-stream provenance checks for same-issue launches that should be allowed.
- Desired Outcome: control-host-launched provider-worker manifests persist the full provenance tuple `provider_launch_source`, `provider_control_host_task_id`, and `provider_control_host_run_id`; older manifests missing that tuple receive truthful same-run backfill; same-issue `linear child-lane` and `linear child-stream` succeed when the recorded manifest provenance matches the active environment; mismatched provenance still fails closed.
