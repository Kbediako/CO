## Scope
- Make `control-host recover --issue-id` use targeted issue-by-id recovery through `dispatch_source_issue_by_id`.
- Ensure a stale Backlog ignored provider-intake claim cannot block or replace targeted issue-by-id truth for that exact recover attempt.
- Preserve tracked-issues discovery constrained behavior for non-targeted discovery, recovery sweeps, and queue refresh.
- Preserve Linear budget, admission caps, provider-intake audit visibility, and provider-worker launch safety.
- Keep CO-281 normal Backlog reclaim, CO-493 Rework metadata recovery, rate-limit policy removal, budget bypass, and manual provider-intake editing out of scope.

## Technical Requirements
- Reproduce targeted `control-host recover --issue-id` when tracked-issues discovery is constrained and stale Backlog ignored provider-intake evidence exists; expected dispatch source is `dispatch_source_issue_by_id`.
- Record targeted issue-by-id provenance in the recovery decision, proof, or diagnostic surface.
- Keep stale Backlog provider-intake evidence visible for audit but non-authoritative for targeted recovery.
- Fail closed with explicit diagnostics when issue-by-id lookup, budget, or recoverability checks fail.
- Add deterministic focused tests without live Linear calls.
