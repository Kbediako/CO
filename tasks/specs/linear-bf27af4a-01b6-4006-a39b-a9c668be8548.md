## Summary
- Objective: make CO doctor/default/init setup safe for Codex CLI `0.125.0` `features.multi_agent_v2=true` thread-limit schema while preserving the normal stable `features.multi_agent=true` baseline.
- Scope:
  - `orchestrator/src/cli/doctor.ts`
  - `orchestrator/src/cli/codexDefaultsSetup.ts`
  - `orchestrator/src/cli/init.ts`
  - `orchestrator/src/cli/utils/codexFeatures.ts`
  - targeted doctor/default/init tests
  - docs/templates that describe `agents.max_threads`
- Constraints:
  - do not remove normal `agents.max_threads = 12` guidance for stable multi-agent sessions
  - do not treat `multi_agent_v2` as default or generally recommended
  - do not expand into unrelated Codex version policy

- Functional requirements:
