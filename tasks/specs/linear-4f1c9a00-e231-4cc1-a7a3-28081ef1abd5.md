## Summary
Require full retained-fallback metadata before provider-worker or review guidance can treat a `legacy succeeded payload` with a preserved `termination_boundary` as successful bounded review completion. This must preserve modern `review/telemetry.json` semantics: `status: succeeded`, `review_outcome: bounded-success`, preserved `termination_boundary`, and semantic `review_verdict` remain separate fields, and clean handoff still requires `review_verdict: clean`.
