## Technical Requirements
- Functional requirements:
  1. Provide a bounded reproduction or simulation of the multi-worker local pressure shape that is representative of the reported `SIGABRT` / `V8 OOM` risk.
  2. Add a local `host-safety` boundary that suppresses new work before local pressure becomes unbounded; active work should remain truthful rather than being killed just to restore headroom.
  3. Ensure repeated `provider_worker_child_lane_provenance_invalid` attempts from the same parent or issue cannot keep spamming progress or retry loops inside the same minute unless relevant state or evidence changes.
  4. Tighten local `provider-worker` and extra-lane admission or launch rules so long-lived workers plus additional lanes obey stricter local `backpressure`.
  5. Expose explicit observability when work is suppressed for `host-safety`, including enough context to distinguish suppression from generic retrying.
  6. Preserve `fail closed` provenance behavior for real mismatches and missing recorded provenance.
  7. Keep the fix local-host scoped and do not weaken retry or delegation guard contracts as a side effect.
- Non-functional requirements (performance, reliability, security):
  - no silent retry broadening
  - no fake healthy progress when CO is actually suppressing work
  - no regression that turns provenance-invalid failures into success or ambiguous retry states
  - machine-checkable reproduction and observability artifacts
