About Project(3)

Obys’ Design Books is a curated collection of titles that shaped how we see, think, and create. Each book is a reminder that design evolves through reflection.

It’s not a library about design — it’s a library for designers. A space where ideas from different decades and disciplines meet in quiet conversation. Together, they form a portrait of design as a living dialogue between the past, the present, and what’s still to come.

Why it matters:

  1. to learn from the masters
  2. to move slower, see deeper
  3. to remain open, inspired
