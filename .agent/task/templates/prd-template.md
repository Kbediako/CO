## Fallback / Refactor Decision
- Applies to fallback, compatibility, legacy, stale, cached, break-glass, or minor-seam behavior? `<Yes|No>`.
- If yes, choose for each touched fallback/seam: `remove fallback`, `expire fallback`, or `justify retaining fallback`.
- For any retained fallback/seam, record owner, trigger, introduced date, review date, allowed maximum lifetime or durable-retention status, removal condition or non-expiring rationale, and validation.
- For `expire fallback`, the maximum lifetime must fit the applicable cap in `docs/guides/fallback-expiry-and-refactor-policy.md`.
- For `justify retaining fallback`, also record contract name, owning surface, proof that it is steady-state behavior, tests/docs that describe it as a supported path, and why it is not governed as an expiring fallback.
- Large-refactor check: explain why another minor seam is acceptable, or name the large-refactor plan/follow-up.
