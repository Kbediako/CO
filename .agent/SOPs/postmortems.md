# Postmortem SOP

## Added by Bootstrap 2025-10-16

1. Convene the response team within 48 hours of incident resolution.
2. Document a blameless timeline, detection gaps, and contributing factors.
3. List corrective actions with owners, due dates, and verification steps.
4. Update `/tasks/index.json` if new specs, tasks, or research notes are required.
5. Track follow-through until every action is completed or reprioritized.
